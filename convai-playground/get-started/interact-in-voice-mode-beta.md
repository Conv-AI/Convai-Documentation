# Interact in Voice Mode (Beta)

### Introduction

Voice Mode enables low-friction, natural conversations with your character. This guide covers the basic setup, choosing an interaction method, starting/stopping voice, and best practices for stable, low-latency sessions.

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### 1. Open Your Character

* From your Dashboard, open the character you want to use with Voice Mode.
* Go to Core AI settings and select a live model.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (439).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### 2. Configure Voice Settings

* Go to Language and Speech, and select your voice.
* Its preferred not to use GCP voices, as they are not compatible with live models.

#### 3. Using Voice Mode

* After successful update of the character, you will observe  a new buttons appears on the chat input.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (440).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* On clicking, you will open the Voice Mode where you can interact with your character handsfree.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (442).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* After an interaction, if you close the voice mode, you will observe your conversation in the chat area.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (443).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* After a 5min afk, the chatbot gets disconnected and you may have to reconnect to continue the interaction.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (444).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
